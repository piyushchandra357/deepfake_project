### Deepfake Detection Challenge Dataset by Facebook AI.
- [Dataset link](https://ai.facebook.com/datasets/dfdc)

- **Preview dataset**

    * Featuring two facial modification algorithms

- **Full dataset**
  * 124k videos
  * Featuring eight facial modification algorithms
